Volta Emergency Help

🚨 Sending SOS

Select your emergency type then tap the red SOS button. Your GPS location and boat details are sent instantly to rescue teams and your saved contacts.

📍 GPS Location

The app uses your device GPS to share your live position. Allow location access when prompted for the fastest response.

📡 Offline Mode

Without internet, the app falls back to sending GPS coordinates only. Always try to stay in network range on the lake.

👥 Emergency Contacts

Save contacts in the Profile tab. They receive an SMS alert with your coordinates every time you press SOS.

🔥 Safety Tips

  • Keep GPS enabled at all times
  • Keep your phone charged before trips
  • Update your passenger count before departure
  • Stay calm — help is on the way
